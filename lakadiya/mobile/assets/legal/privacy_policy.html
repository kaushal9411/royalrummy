Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Lakadiya — Real-Money Card Game Platform

This Privacy Policy describes how Lakadiya ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Lakadiya mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: Mobile phone number, username, email address (optional), date of birth (required for age verification)
  • KYC: PAN card number, full legal name, PAN card document image, selfie photograph (required before first withdrawal)
  • Payment: Transaction history, wallet balance. Card/UPI details are processed by Razorpay and not stored on our servers.
  • Device: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications, device type
  • Game Data: Match history, scores, XP, level, game statistics
  • Usage: In-app activity, IP address, session information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process payments and withdrawals via Razorpay
  • To verify your identity and age (18+ requirement)
  • To prevent fraud and money laundering
  • To send OTP codes for authentication
  • To send transactional notifications (payment receipts, withdrawal status)
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations (RBI guidelines, PMLA)

3. Age Restriction

Lakadiya is strictly restricted to users 18 years of age or older. We collect date of birth during registration to verify compliance. Users found to be under 18 will have their accounts terminated and funds returned.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With Razorpay for payment processing
  • With Google Firebase for authentication and push notifications
  • With law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit and AES-256 encryption for sensitive stored credentials. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian financial regulations. You may request deletion by contacting privacy@lakadiya.in.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and non-legally-required data
  • Opt out of promotional notifications in Notification Settings

8. Responsible Gaming

We support responsible gaming. You can set spending limits and self-exclude from the platform at any time in the Settings screen. For problem gambling help, contact the National Problem Gambling Helpline.

9. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics for anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected by Crashlytics.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests: privacy@lakadiya.in

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
