Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025
Lakadiya — Real-Money Card Game Platform

This Privacy Policy describes how Lakadiya ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Lakadiya mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Mobile phone number, username, email address (optional), date of birth (required for age verification)
  • KYC Information: PAN card number, full legal name, PAN card document image, selfie photograph (required before first withdrawal)
  • Payment Information: Payment transaction history, wallet balance, withdrawal requests. Card/UPI details are processed by Razorpay and are not stored on our servers.
  • Device Information: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications, device type (Android/iOS)
  • Game Data: Match history, scores, XP, level, game statistics
  • Usage Data: In-app activity, IP address, session information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process payments and withdrawals via Razorpay
  • To verify your identity and age (18+ requirement) as required by applicable law
  • To prevent fraud, money laundering, and underage gambling
  • To send OTP codes for authentication via SMS and push notification
  • To send transactional notifications (payment receipts, withdrawal status)
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations (RBI guidelines, PMLA)

3. Age Restriction

Lakadiya is strictly restricted to users who are 18 years of age or older. We collect date of birth during registration to verify compliance. Users found to be under 18 will have their accounts terminated and any funds returned.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With Razorpay for payment processing (their privacy policy applies to payment data)
  • With Google Firebase for authentication and push notifications
  • With law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit, AES-256 encryption for sensitive stored credentials, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us at support@lakadiya.in — transaction records required by law will be retained regardless.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and non-legally-required data
  • Opt out of promotional notifications (transactional notifications cannot be disabled)

8. Responsible Gaming

We are committed to responsible gaming. You can set daily, weekly, and monthly spending limits and self-exclude from the platform at any time from the Settings screen in the app. If you need help with problem gambling, contact the National Problem Gambling Helpline.

9. Cookies and Analytics

Our mobile app does not use browser cookies. We use Firebase Crashlytics for anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected by Crashlytics.
